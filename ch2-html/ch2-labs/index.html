Mahomet, IL, USA

Location

The town of Mahomet, IL is a small town located in Central Illinois. It is approximately 15 miles from the City of Champaign-Urbana, which is the 9th largest city in Illinois and 4th most populous outside of the Chicago-Metro area. This area is most well known for being the home of the University of Illinois Champaign-Urbana, a prestigious engineering University and research institution.

About

Weather

The weather in Mahomet, IL mimics normal Central IL weather patterns, moderately temperate and experiences all seasons. Winters with heavy snowfall are not uncommon, as well as very hot, humid summers. Spring and Fall are generally very cool.

Population

Population of Mahomet, IL as of 2020 is 9,859. However, trends show the population increasing sharply over the last decade. As Champaign-Urbana grows in size and population, Mahomet, IL has become a popular place for families to move to. Mahomet, IL has the highest median income of cities in Champaign County, IL.

Sports

As Mahomet, IL is a small town, it does not have any professional or collegiate sports teams. However, the residents are very passionate about their local school municipality's sports teams. Mahomet, IL schools are known as the "Bulldogs".

Things To Do

    • River Bend Forest Preserve

  • Attend a local "Bulldogs" sporting event
